MAMA GOOMAN'S CHOCOLATE

"The home of chocolate"

We have the best chocolate business! Chocolate is a key ingredient in many foods such as milk shakes, candy bars, cookies and cereals. It is ranked as one of the most favourite flavours in the world. Chocolate is a product that requires complex procedures to make. The process involves harvesting coca, refining coca to cocoa beans, and shipping the cocoa beans to the manufacturing factory for grinding. These cocoa beans will then be imported or exported to other countries and be transformed into different type of chocolate products.

Our Chocolate is better than all else because we put our soul into our chocolate. Mama Goomans chocolate ensures that every bit is a sensation of happiness. The sweet taste of Mama Goomans is derived from the production of chocolate right from our own coca farms and hand grinding.
